About Us

About Us

Personal care for your healthy living

Physiocare Limited was established in the year 2010 and was incorporated in 2011. We are bases at Elgon Court, Block A, Suite A2 along Ralph Bunche road opposite accident Annex. The company provides physiotherapy and Rehabilitation services besides supply and sale of both Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation equipment and appliances. Our company is in pursuit of attaining excellence in our field through our dedicated professional and sales team. We are committed to provide international quality equipment and appliances at reasonable prices and we shall continue from ISO Certified Manufacturers.

Our Mission

To source, supply and service high quality Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation services, equipment and appliances.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic physiotherapy and rehabilitation providers in the region to both public and private institutions.

Our Core Values

Proactiveness, dedication and exposure to Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation requirements to modern efficient and affordable equipment .
